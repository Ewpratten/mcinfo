About this website

MCInfo is a website built by Evan Pratten to showcase the RetryLife API's Minecraft functionality, and as some web development practice. Everything on this site should be free for ever, no personal data is tracked, and I am not planning to put ads here either. If you would like to make a small donation to support further development of this free project and others, feel free to send something via PayPal Donations, or use one of the cypto addresses listed on my website.
